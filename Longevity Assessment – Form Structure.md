# **Longevity Assessment – Form Structure**

## **Overview**

This form is designed to collect structured health and lifestyle data in order to generate a personalized longevity and performance optimization plan using AI.

The structure is divided into clear sections to create a guided, premium user experience.

---

# **Form Title**

**Longevity Assessment**

## **Description**

Answer a few questions to receive a personalized longevity and performance optimization plan.

---

# **Section 1 — Profile**

**1\. Age**

* Type: Short answer

**2\. Gender**

* Type: Multiple choice  
* Options:  
  * Male  
  * Female  
  * Prefer not to say

---

# **Section 2 — Goals**

**3\. What are your top 2–3 goals right now?**

* Type: Checkboxes  
* Options:  
  * Improve energy  
  * Sleep better  
  * Lose fat  
  * Build muscle  
  * Improve focus  
  * Longevity / aging well  
  * Stress reduction

---

# **Section 3 — Current Symptoms**

**4\. Which of the following do you currently experience?**

* Type: Checkboxes  
* Options:  
  * Low energy  
  * Poor sleep  
  * Brain fog  
  * Stress / anxiety  
  * Weight gain  
  * Low motivation  
  * Digestive issues

---

# **Section 4 — Lifestyle**

**5\. How would you rate your sleep quality?**

* Type: Linear scale (1–10)  
* Labels:  
  * 1 \= Very poor  
  * 10 \= Excellent

**6\. How would you rate your daily energy levels?**

* Type: Linear scale (1–10)

**7\. How would you rate your stress levels?**

* Type: Linear scale (1–10)

**8\. How often do you exercise?**

* Type: Multiple choice  
* Options:  
  * Never  
  * 1–2x per week  
  * 3–4x per week  
  * 5+ times per week

**9\. Which best describes your diet?**

* Type: Multiple choice  
* Options:  
  * Mostly processed foods  
  * Mixed diet  
  * Mostly whole foods  
  * Very clean / structured

---

# **Section 5 — Daily Habits**

**10\. How much caffeine do you consume daily?**

* Type: Multiple choice  
* Options:  
  * None  
  * 1 coffee  
  * 2–3 coffees  
  * 4+

**11\. How often do you consume alcohol?**

* Type: Multiple choice  
* Options:  
  * None  
  * Occasionally  
  * Weekly  
  * Frequently

**12\. Are you currently taking any supplements?**

* Type: Multiple choice  
* Options:  
  * No  
  * Yes (basic: vitamins, magnesium, etc.)  
  * Yes (advanced stack)

---

# **Final Question**

**13\. What is your biggest frustration with your health right now?**

* Type: Short answer  
* Optional

---

# **Design Principles**

* Structured into sections for a guided experience  
* Uses mostly multiple choice and scales for speed  
* Keeps completion time under 5 minutes  
* Balances quantitative and qualitative inputs

---

# **Purpose**

This intake feeds into an AI system that:

* Analyzes user data  
* Identifies key health priorities  
* Generates a personalized longevity protocol
